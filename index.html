Muchachas F.C.

One team. Nine players. One Dream.

The team was created in January 2023 and is playing it's first league game on May 15th.

The main objetive for this season is the World Cup organized in London on June 4th.

World Cup London!

This is our first big event and the team is ready to represent Argentina in the 9-a-side football competition.

More than 15 National teams will fight for this title but Muchachas is ready to fight until the end!
